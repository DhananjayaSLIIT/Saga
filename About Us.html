ABOUT US




Identifying the space for a trusted delivery service, SAGA eats was set up in order to provide a quick and reliable service in delivering foods since 2007. We thought of touching the hearts of our customer becoming their most trusted network in delivering food.
At SAGA Eats, we are focused in providing a trusted and fast delivery service to our beloved customers with a firm belief that customer is our strength. We are always dedicated to provide our best service to them. Currently we are operating in Kandy, Colombo, Kurunegala, Avissawella and sooner we will be expanding our services to Galle, Nuwara-Eliya, Jaffna and Anuradhapura. Our objective to be there when you need our services.

Responsibility for quality service


We will deliver excellence and respond vigorously to change. Each of us is responsible for the quality of whatever we do.

Deliver customer satisfaction


We are dedicated to satisfying our customers by listening to their requests and understanding their expectations.
